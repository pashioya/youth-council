Who are we?

Bataljong is a membership organisation, and we like to extend that into the structure of our organisation. Both our Board of Directors and the General Assembly are composed of members, supplemented by professionals and interesting people from our partner organisations.

What do we do

As a member organization of cities and municipalities, we are committed to strong policy for young residents. We connect and inspire youth councilors, youth officials and aldermen of youth to work on this together. Bataljong also promotes the advantages of child and youth-friendly policy at Flemish, federal and European level, and thereby represents the interests of cities and municipalities.

Our Values

Justice

Justice is a basic value for Bataljong that is in line with our struggle for a full place for children and young people in local policy, based on children's rights.

Communication

Bataljong will work as a connecting network organization. In a city or municipality, we connect different target groups around a transversal story.

Leadership

Bataljong dares to play the first note and set the tone as an innovator and pacesetter of local policy for children and young people.

Why should you join?

As a member of our platform, you'll be able to engage with your peers on topics that matter to you, share your own ideas, and get involved in the decision-making process in your community. Our online survey feature will allow you to elicit themes from other young people in your local area, giving you a chance to understand what issues are important to them.
